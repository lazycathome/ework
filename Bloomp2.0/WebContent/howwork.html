Register with just your phone number and your name.
  Click the "+" sign at the top right corner of the app to create a new task. You can either set a due time or use a timer. Add executor(s) and onlooker(s) (who can watch the executor's progress).
  You can add contacts who already have Bloomp, or invite those in your contact book to download Bloomp. Only friends can execute/be an onlooker for your tasks.
  Click on the balloons in the "To-Do" tab to observe your progress. You can use the chat icon in the top right corner to message everyone involved in the task.
  To complete a task, just tap the balloon and then tap "complete"! The task will then appear in your History.
